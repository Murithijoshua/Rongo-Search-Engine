Vacancies 




We are home to a diverse cohort of local and international academic, professional and support staff who all strive to ensure that we continue to be renowned for our quality, relevance and impact. Lifelong learning is what we are about.Rongo University is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.

						Application Guidelines					


						Applicants should forward their applications including a soft copy and detailed Curriculum Vitae that should indicate their primary research interests, full personal details, publications and scholarly activities. They should attach supporting certified copies of certificates and testimonials. Applicants should also indicate their current earning and salary expectations. Applicants should provide day time telephone number and names and addresses of three referees who are knowledgeable about applicants’ competence, area of specialization, character and faith. The applicants should also request their referees to write immediately and directly to the undersigned.
