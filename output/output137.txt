Tenders 












				Rongo University ensures that the Kenyan economy is supported, and from time to time we request the services of suitable providers to apply for relevant tenders. We have very strict procurement policies and procedures which are in line with legal requirements and the University’s commitment to ethical practices. All the relevant information requests are contained within each call for proposals or providers of goods and services.					



















 




						Take Note					


						•It is the responsibility of service providers to ensure that all required documentation is valid for each submission

•The University reserves its right to remove a tender or cancel a tender without any notice or reason

•The University reserves the right not to award the tenders

•No late submissi
ons will be accepted.
