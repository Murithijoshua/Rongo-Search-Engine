Data Analysis to Establish Trends, Root Causes Consequences & Prevention:
The Centre plays a critical in looking at the trends, roots and consequences of crime with a view to addressing crime on the basis of empirical action-oriented research. The Centre undertakes impartial and contemporary policy-relevant research of the highest standard. Data analysis is undertaken using Statistical Package for Social Sciences (SPSS), Qlik, EnVivo, Ms. Excel among other statistical data analysis softwares.
