However campaign posters may be put on noticeboards, posts, fences, trees or portable display boards and strings. FOR POSTERS DISPLAYED OUTSIDE UNIVERSITY PREMISES MAY REQUIRE CONSENT OF COUNTY GOVERNMENT OR OWNERS OF THOSE FACILITIES. Dr. J.M. Mulwa CHAIRMAN, STUDENTS ELECTORAL COMMISSION
