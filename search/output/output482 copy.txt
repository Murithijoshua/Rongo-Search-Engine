Welcome to the Center for Service Learning and Volunteerism!  
Welcome to the Center for Service Learning and Volunteerism (CSLV)! The CSLV provides
 services to students, faculty, staff and community partners to encourage community
 service-learning that is mutually beneficial to the university and our local community.
 
To get started or learn more about how to volunteer, visit our How to Serve Page or watch a quick video on how to use our volunteer platform, Volunteer Odyssey.
 
 
Mission and Purpose
 
Mission:
The mission of the Center for Service Learning and Volunteerism is to provide students,
 faculty, and staff with meaningful service opportunities and to develop them into
 good stewards and change agents in their local, regional, and global communities. 
 
Purpose:
The purpose of the Center for Service Learning and Volunteerism is to serve as a liaison
 between faculty, students, and community partners. We offer support, resources, and
 placement opportunities to empower students' experiential learning through a focus
 on civic engagement and social responsibility. 
 
Core Values: 
 
Celebrate: Diversity, Growth, Innovation, Each Other
 
Cultivate: Kindness, Courage, Connections, Love of Learning
 
Prepare: Leaders, Responsible Citizens, Lifelong Learners, Agents of Change
 
 
By the Numbers

During the 2019-2020 School Year, over 3,400 students volunteered over 20,000 hours to yield an economic impact of $450,350 back to the greater Memphis Community (economic impact is a measure of the value
 of volunteer time by The Independent Sector).
 
Ready to start volunteering? Get started through TigerZone or Volunteer Odyssey.
