Leadership and Policy Studies
The Leadership and Policy Studies (LDPS) Program is among the leading academic programs
 in the country offering opportunities to study with nationally- and internationally-renowned
 faculty in K-12 administration and policy analysis.
 
If you are a practitioner looking to deepen your knowledge and strengthen your credentials,
 our Master of Science (MS) and Doctor of Education (EdD) degree programs will prepare
 you for leadership roles in a variety of leadership roles in K-12 schools, government
 agencies, corporations, and nongovernmental organizations.
 
We invite you to explore more about our degree offerings and to get to know our faculty
 members. Should you require additional assistance, please contact the Department of
 Leadership directly at:
 
Leadership and Policy Studies ProgramDepartment of LeadershipUniversity of Memphis123 Ball HallMemphis, TN 38152(901) 678-2369Email: ldps@memphis.edu
LDPS Degree and Program Offerings

Master of Science (MS) in Leadership and Policy Studies: School Administration and
 Supervision
Doctor of Education (EdD) with emphasis in School Leadership
Doctor of Education (EdD) with emphasis in Policy Studies
