 Explore + Discover

Memphis

In a city that has changed the world in more ways than one, we're driven to do the
 same.  And so can you.  The University of Memphis has been educating students from
 all over the world for more than 100 years.  UofM researchers and faculty are leaders
 in over 250 fields of study, which means a wealth of academic choices for you.  In
 the heart of the nation's 20th largest city, learning isn't limited to classrooms
 or labs.  Memphis is a hub of international commerce, culture and creativity.  Whatever
 your passion, here you'll discover endless paths toward pursuing it.
