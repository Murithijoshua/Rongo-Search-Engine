Professional MBA
(PMBA)

The Professional MBA program offered by the Fogelman College of Business & Economics
 is designed for ambitious full-time professionals who are driven to reach greater
 heights in their profession.
 
This program is designed to accommodate those who are interested in taking classes
 part-time to accommodate their busy schedules, and  those who are seeking to complete
 their degrees full-time.
 
The Professional MBA program offers:
Flexibility
Class scheduling is optimized for working schedules and offers a mixture of classroom-based
 evening sections and online sections. Students may enroll on a full-time or part-time
 basis.
 
Quality

Our MBA program is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of
 Business (AACSB), a quality standard achieved by only 5% of business schools worldwide.
 
Affordability
Our MBA program is exceptionally price-competitive.
Specialization
Our program offers the opportunity to obtain a specialized MBA degree by completing
 a concentration.
