Graduate Program
Thank you for your interest in the graduate programs in the Department of Physics and Materials Science.
PhD Program
The department offers PhD in Applied Physics with Financial Support in these four
 focus areas:
 

Solid State Materials
Soft Matter
Computational Physics
Astrophysics

For application information and how to apply to the PhD program, please click here.
MS Program
The department also offers MS in Physics in the following concentrations:

General Physics
Materials Science
Computational Physics

For application information and how to apply to the MS program, please click here. 
For additional information about our graduate programs, please contact the graduate
 coordinator, Dr. Xiao Shen, at + 1 901.678.1668 or xshen1@memphis.edu.
