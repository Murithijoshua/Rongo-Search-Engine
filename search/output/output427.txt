Strengthening Student Connections to the University
Welcome to The University of Memphis Division of Student Academic Success! Our purpose
 is to help students persist to graduation by providing programs and services that
 help you achieve your academic, career and belonging goals.
 
Please read a message about remote services from our Vice President for Student Academic Success Dr. Karen Weddle-West and Dr.
 Tom Nenon, Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs and Provost regarding services
 from our departments as we transition to online and remote classes and services.
 

Departments in Student Academic Success connect students and their families to the resources needed to navigate the opportunities
 and challenges that will come your way. The UofM is a wonderfully diverse and inclusive
 campus and we are committed to helping you discover the ways you can make your experience
 here as meaningful and exciting as possible.
 
Follow us for the latest updates on all things Student Success!
