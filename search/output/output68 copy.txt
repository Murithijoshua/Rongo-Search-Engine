Partners in Innovation
Welcome to the intersection of research and applied innovation at the University of
 Memphis. Whether you are a corporation, a community organization, or a startup looking
 to take the world by storm, the Division of Research and Innovation is ready to work
 with you to help achieve your goals. The opportunities for collaboration with the
 University of Memphis are multifaceted; real impact starts right here.
 
 
FedEx Institute of Technology
The FedEx Institute of Technology is the home for applied innovation at the University
 of Memphis and represents a unique public-private partnership among Fortune 500 companies.
 Through the FedEx Institute of Technology's strategic interdisciplinary focus on emerging
 technologies, Memphis is building a wave of applied research and situating the UofM
 as the strategic solution for advanced technology workforce solutions.
 
Learn More | Research Clusters
 
Office of Technology Transfer
The Office of Technology Transfer is responsible for the process of transferring scientific
 findings from one organization to another for the purpose of further development and
 commercialization. The Office assists researchers with filing patents and invention
 disclosures, helps with the licensing of University IP, and expands the impact of
 the University's research products.
 
Learn More
 
UMRF Research Park
The University of Memphis is pleased to provide a landing pad for growth stage and
 mature technology companies ready to call Memphis home. The UMRF Research Park is
 a part of the University of Memphis Research Foundation, a 501c3 committed to growing
 the research capacity for the entire campus. Providing aligned resources and office
 facilities for collaborators, The UMRF Research Park helps companies from around the
 world see the value of operating in Memphis.
 
Learn More
 
Partnerships
Whether you are looking for research, workforce development solutions, or collaboration
 to advance Memphis as a center of innovation, we are open to working with you. Partnership
 opportunities for organizations of all types are available; we can help you find the
 right teams at the University of Memphis to get you where you want to go.
 
Learn More
