 Research Administration & Business Operations
 
 


 
 Research Administration & Business Operations is comprised of Environmental Health
 & Safety, Grants & Contracts, Housing & Residence Life, Parking & Transportation Services,
 Rental Properties, and the Shared Services Center. 
 
 





 
 Office of the Chief Financial Officer
 
 


 
 Oversees the Division of Business & Finance and provides direct supervision of Financial
 Planning & Analysis, Procurement & Contract Services, and University & Student Business
 Services
 
 





 
 Campus Planning & Design
 
 


 
 Responsible for the planning, design, and implementation of facility and site improvements.
 
 





 
 Financial Accounting & Reporting
 
 


 
 Oversees the operations of the Payroll and Accounting offices and works closely with
 Financial Planning and University & Student Business Services
 
 





 
 Human Resources
 
 


 
 HR serves as a business partner for the University community by consistently championing
 relevant and meaningful HR issues that address the growing needs of our dynamic institution
 
 





 
 Physical Plant
 
 


 
 Charged with the operation and maintenance of the physical facilities as well as the
 landscape of the University

Welcome to the Division of Business and Finance!
The Division of Business and Finance is responsible for the management of University
 fiscal and human resources. Business & Finance is one of the major divisions within
 the University of Memphis, a public institution of higher education located in the
 heart of Memphis. Our 100+ full-time employees provide service and support to approximately
 23,000 students and more than 2,500 faculty and staff.
