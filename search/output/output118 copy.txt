Openstax
Openstax is a nonprofit dedicated to improving access to educational materials. Based at Rice University,
 Openstax provides free access to textbooks and other educational resources. Openstax
 maintains a collection of online textbooks that are freely available for faculty to
 adopt for use in their classes. The openstax materials are also freely available to
 be used as supplemental materials by students who would benefit from additional materials
 to support their studies. More information is available on the Openstax website at
 https://openstax.org or on-campus contact  Dr. John Evans, Executive Director of University Libraries.
