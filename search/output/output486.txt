 	UofM Media Room
 	

Your source for UofM news.
Did you know no two days at the UofM are the same? That's because our faculty, staff
 and students are always conducting groundbreaking research, composing new music, performing
 theatrical works of art, hosting events to bring our Tiger family together and just
 doing. Alumni and community partners are investing in our students' futures through new
 building development, new scholarship and training opportunities and advanced research
 funds.
 
The UofM Media Room is home to everything you need to know about University happenings.
 Read the latest edition of UofM Magazine, share recent news releases and view upcoming events.
 
Have an idea for a story to be featured in the magazine? Has your department recently
 been honored? Let us know! Email us at prnews@memphis.edu.
