 	Marketing and Communication
 	

Raising the UofM profile through strategic marketing.
 Our department coordinates the development of integrated marketing and communication
 strategies and programs to reach the University's target audiences. Our team specializes
 in the following areas:

Marketing - seeks to increase enrollment and raise the profiles of departments and offices
 across campus through advertising and outreach campaigns
 
Communication - tells the newsworthy stories of endeavors and research on campus through traditional
 and digital media at the local and national levels
 
Creative Services - ensures each department represents the University's brand standards by providing
 professional marketing materials for clients across campus
 
Photography and Videography - documents campus life to share the University's stories
 
Social Media - shares news, information and campus highlights through digital media platforms
 
The University of Memphis Magazine - publishes two print issues of the official magazine and one President's report annually
 
University Special Events - coordinates premiere University events and provides resources for events hosted
 by UofM departments
 

Our focus is primarily on the University's academic, administrative and service units.
 We work in concert with Athletics on projects and efforts affecting the entire University
 community and branding for Memphis Tigers sports.
