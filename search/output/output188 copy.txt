A Personalized Learning Environment. A Straight Path to Success.
The University of Memphis Lambuth is located on a tranquil 57-acre campus in the heart of Jackson, TN. Benefit from small class sizes and experienced faculty members who enable hands-on learning in a close-knit community-with all the backing of a major research university.
At UofM Lambuth, your learning possibilities are endless. What are you waiting for? Get started on your amazing journey with us today!
