Why Pursue Graduate Studies in Political Science at the University of Memphis?

Our graduates are successful, as our program is an effective springboard into both
 academic and non-academic careers.
 
Our assistantships cover all fees and tuition, and stipends are quite competitive
 with other graduate programs.
 
Our students develop a critical and versatile skill-set.
We have an excellent student-to-faculty ratio in our graduate program.
We offer a well-rounded curriculum.
Our program has national visibility.

Click here to learn why graduate studies in Political Science may be right for you.
