Hospitality and Resort Management 
The Kemmons Wilson School of Hospitality and Resort Management (KWS) at The University
 of Memphis was created and named after the founder of Holiday Inns, Kemmons Wilson,
 Sr. KWS offers a balance of academics and experiential learning in the hospitality,
 resort, and tourism business stressing global awareness, diversity and sustainability.
 
Our program provides an education stemming from the roots of hospitality and is designed
 to prepare students for a 21st century career in one of the largest industries of
 the world. Field experience provides students with learning while working in a variety
 of settings, such as: InterContinental Hotel Group properties, Marriott Hotels and
 Resorts, Hilton Hotels, Peabody Hotel Group, Caesars Entertainment, and a myriad of
 other hotels, restaurants, resorts, sport and tourism businesses. Career opportunities
 abound in this fastest growing of all fields, and KWS graduates will be prepared to
 assume their roles in the business. Learn more about career opportunities >

Degree Opportunities



Undergraduate
Earn a Bachelor of Arts to be successful in the highly competitive hospitality business
 environment. 
 


Graduate
Earn a Masters of Science through our traditional 33-credit hour graduate program.


Graduate Certificate
Establish industry credentials with our 12-credit program comprised of four required
 courses. The program can be completed in less than one year when taken part-time.
 


Minor
Choose to minor in Hospitality and Resort Management to further increase your skills
 and portfolio.
