STEP UP: Start a Master's or PhD Today
Are you looking to take the next step in your career? Thinking about a new profession?
 Or would you like to brush up on new advancements in your field? At the University
 of Memphis, we can help you continue your drive and enhance the knowledge and experience
 you already possess. Our top-ranked, affordable and accredited graduate programs are
 designed to benefit both full-time students and working professionals. As a major
 urban research university, the UofM enrolls roughly 4,900 graduate students in more
 than 161 concentrations.
