Graduate Students
The Department of English offers the following graduate degrees and concentrations:


MA in English, with a concentration in one of the following:

Applied Linguistics
Literature
Technical Communication
TESOL (Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages) [both in traditional and completely
 online form]
 
Writing and Rhetoric




MFA in Creative Writing


PhD in English, with a concentration in one of the following:

Applied Linguistics
Literary and Cultural Studies
Writing, Rhetoric, and Technical Communication 




Certificate Programs:

African-American Literature
Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages 
Writing Studies
