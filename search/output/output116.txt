 African American Male Academy
 
 


 
 Through the African American Male Academy, the UofM will foster a culture of inclusive
 excellence for African American male students, with an end goal of college completion.

 	Corporate and Foundation Relations
 	

Together, there are no limits.
For our corporate and foundation partners, we offer a "one-stop shop" for University
 engagement. Whether through student engagement, technology transfer and commercialization,
 sponsored research, or executive education, we strive to build mutually beneficial
 partnerships that align business goals and initiatives with the University's most
 dynamic resources: our exceptional students and distinguished faculty. Our office
 helps organizations navigate the university, identify opportunities for collaboration
 and connect with faculty and programs of interest.
