Current Status






The following actions and policies are in place effective July 28. All updates will
 be posted to this site. 
 
Masks are required to be worn by all persons while indoors and in places where maintaining
 appropriate social distancing is not possible.
 
All students, faculty and staff are strongly encouraged to receive the COVID-19 vaccine.
 
The University has 100% of our staff back to a regular on-campus schedule. Our campus
 facilities (residence halls, dining outlets, student recreation center, etc.) are
 open and offer a full range of activities and the traditional higher education experience
 that our students expect.
 


 Read All Updates > 








Health & Wellness





Stay safe, Tigers.

UofM is committed to keeping the campus community healthy while learning, living,
 teaching and working.
 
All students, faculty and staff are strongly encouraged to receive the COVID-19 vaccine.
Effective July 28, masks are required to be worn by all persons while indoors and
 in places where maintaining appropriate social distancing is not possible.
 
Daily symptom monitoring and self-reporting of positive test results are required.
