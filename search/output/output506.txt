Visit the Campus
We’re so glad you’re interested in the University of Memphis and can’t wait to have
 you on our main campus in Memphis, Tennessee! Tigers Together. Every Tiger has a role to play in protecting our campus community and preventing the
 spread of COVID-19. Explore this site to see what UofM is doing and learn how you can do you part while on campus. Masks are required indoors and where social distancing isn't possible.





Campus Tours for Individual Prospective Undergraduate Students



Click here to schedule a campus tour! 
If you are unable to attend a campus tour or an in-person visit, we encourage you
 to explore our virtual options.
 











Housing Tours for Individual Prospective Undergraduate Students



We are excited to host housing tours for prospective undergraduate students! Be sure
 to click the "Housing Tour" option when scheduling a campus tour.Click here to schedule a campus tour!  
 







Department Meetings for Individual Prospective Undergraduate Students



Click here to learn more about adding a department meeting to your tour!





Campus Tours for School Groups



Elementary and Middle School: We are excited to offer an engaging scavenger hunt for all of our elementary and middle
 school aged vistors. Groups will meet at Wilder Tower to begin the scavenger hunt
 where students will learn about campus answering prompts related to important campus
 figures and buildings. Click here to schedule a scavenger hunt. High School: We are excited to offer a 60-minute walking tour for our high school groups. Our tour
 guides will lead students across campus starting and ending at Wilder Tower. Click
 here to schedule a high school campus tour.
 













Self-Guided Campus Tours



Follow this tour script for a self-guided tour around campus! 
 






RSVP for Special Events



Throughout the year, the Office of Admissions and Orientation organizes events both
 on and off campus. We invite you to visit our schedule of events page often to stay up-to-date on recruitment events that may be of interest to you.
 





Traveling to the UofM



Coming from out of town? Visitors to the University of Memphis are eligible for special
 rates with the Holiday Inn-University of Memphis. Please call 901-678-8200 to book your stay with a special discount. Please note
 that rates are based on hotel availability and are unavailable during high-occupancy,
 sold-out or blackout dates.
